Our Mission

With our combined business backgrounds that range from from commercial realestate to finance to renewable energy to information system implementation to sales and customer acquisition to video production and marketing, and with our combined love of the wilderness, travel, and learning about local cultures, we strive to help small businesses in the local communities grow and thrive so they can continue to support the surrounding wonders of the world that so many people across the world enjoy every year. We believe it is important to give back that is why we give 5% of our profit to 3rd party non-profit organizations dedicated to protecting the environment.
